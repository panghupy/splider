Responsibilities:

•Cooperate with R&D food technologist to write product specification, nutrition data, ingredient declaration, range formula and issue relevant information that customer requested based on NWF policy and regulation.

职责：

配合研发食品工程师写产品说明，营养成份数据，成分说明，规定范围内的配方和纽利味政策和法规规定范围内的客户要求的相关信息。


•Maintain proper computer and filing documentation for specification and all information that release to customer or government. 

保管适当的电子版本和文件版本的数据信息以便提供给客户或政府。


•Work with R&D technologist and Q.C to ensure all ingredients in the formula as well as products can be meet Chinese foods regulation and customer requirement. 

配合研发人员和质检人员的工作确保所有配方和产品中的成分符合中国食品法规和客户的要求。


•Work with purchasing and Q.C to verify new ingredient and supplier qualification.

配合采购和质检人员核实新的成分和供应商资质。


•Prepare and provide regulation related technical information services to customers. Maintain proper technical information communication with internal and external customers.

为客户准备和提供法规相关技术信息服务。维护内外部客户的技术信息沟通。


•Support sales, production, R&D, Quality and Compliance Department. 

支持销售、生产、研发、质量和合规部。



•Report to Senior Compliance Manager

报告给合规经理。



工作地址：

北京市怀柔区雁栖工业开发区雁栖北二街11号

纽利味食品(北京)有限公司 该公司其他职位

Newly Weds Foods ( Beijing ) Co., Ltd. (NWFB) is a wholly foreign-funded enterprise invested by Newly Weds Foods Inc., (NWF) a venerable and reputable American industrial food ingredient company. NWF was incorporated in 1932 with headquarter in Chicago . Over the past half century, NWF has become a world leader in providing a full range of services and ingredient products coating for the food processing industry.

In recognizing that China and Southeast Asia are important strategic markets for food ingredients, seasonings and food coatings, NWF China has a production facility in Beijing with regional sales force cover all regional areas of China . Other NWF Asia Pacific regional companies include Thai Food Coatings ( Bangkok ) and New Food Coatings (Sydney) and the New Food Coatings ( Philippines ). The business scope and capabilities of Newly Weds Foods China focus mainly on customize coating systems (customized batters & breading / customized bread crumbs / cracker meals) and customize functional ingredients, seasonings blends and ingredient blends.
